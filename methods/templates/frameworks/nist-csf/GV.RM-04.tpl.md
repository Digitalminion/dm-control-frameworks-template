## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-04 Outcome

**Strategic risk response options are determined and prioritized**

Organizations systematically identify, evaluate, and prioritize cybersecurity risk response strategies (accept, avoid, transfer, mitigate) based on comprehensive analysis of costs, benefits, effectiveness, and alignment with business objectives, risk appetite, and organizational capabilities to optimize risk management resource allocation and strategic outcomes.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling informed, evidence-based decision-making on cybersecurity risk treatment strategies
- Optimizing resource allocation and investment in risk mitigation and management activities
- Aligning risk response strategies with organizational objectives, capabilities, and risk tolerance
- Supporting systematic prioritization of risk management activities across the enterprise
- Demonstrating strategic approach to risk management for stakeholders and regulatory compliance
