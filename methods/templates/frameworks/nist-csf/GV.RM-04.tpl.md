## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-04 Outcome

**Strategic risk response options are determined and prioritized**

Organizations identify, evaluate, and prioritize cybersecurity risk response strategies (e.g., mitigate, transfer, avoid, accept) to align with business objectives and risk appetite.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling informed decision-making on risk treatment actions
- Aligning risk responses with enterprise objectives and resources
- Facilitating efficient allocation of mitigation budgets
- Improving transparency with stakeholders regarding risk posture
- Supporting continuous improvement of risk management processes
