### GV.RM-01 Outcome

**Risk management objectives are established and agreed to by organizational stakeholders**

Organizations develop, communicate, and gain stakeholder agreement on clear risk management objectives that align with organizational strategy and support mission achievement through effective cybersecurity risk governance.

- Establishing clear direction and priorities for cybersecurity risk management activities
- Ensuring alignment between cybersecurity investments and organizational strategic objectives
