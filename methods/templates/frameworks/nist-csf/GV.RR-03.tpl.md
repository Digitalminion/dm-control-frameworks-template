## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RR-03 Outcome

**Adequate resources are allocated commensurate with the cybersecurity risk strategy, roles, responsibilities, and policies**

Organizations establish comprehensive resource allocation frameworks to ensure cybersecurity risk management receives adequate people, process, and technology resources aligned with strategic priorities, risk tolerance, and organizational capabilities for effective risk management and operational sustainability.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity risk management capabilities are adequately resourced for effective operation
- Aligning resource allocation with strategic priorities, risk tolerance, and business objectives
- Supporting sustainable cybersecurity operations and continuous capability improvement
- Demonstrating organizational commitment to cybersecurity through appropriate investment
- Enabling effective execution of cybersecurity roles, responsibilities, and policies
