## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OC-04 Outcome

**Critical objectives, capabilities, and services that stakeholders depend on or expect from the organization are understood and communicated**

Organizations identify, assess, and clearly communicate their critical objectives, capabilities, and services to ensure stakeholder dependencies are understood and cybersecurity protections are appropriately prioritized.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity investments align with critical business objectives and stakeholder dependencies
- Providing clear prioritization criteria for cybersecurity resource allocation
- Enabling stakeholder communication about cybersecurity protection priorities
- Supporting business continuity planning and resilience strategy development
- Facilitating risk-informed decision making based on criticality assessment
