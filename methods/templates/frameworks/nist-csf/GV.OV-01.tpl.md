## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OV-01 Outcome

**Cybersecurity risk management strategy outcomes are reviewed to inform and adjust strategy and direction**

Organizations establish systematic processes to review cybersecurity risk management strategy outcomes, assess their effectiveness against organizational objectives, and make informed adjustments to strategy and direction based on evidence and performance data.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity strategies remain aligned with organizational objectives and threat landscape
- Providing evidence-based decision making for strategic adjustments and resource allocation
- Demonstrating accountability and oversight to stakeholders and leadership
- Enabling continuous improvement in cybersecurity risk management effectiveness
- Supporting organizational learning and adaptation in dynamic threat environments
