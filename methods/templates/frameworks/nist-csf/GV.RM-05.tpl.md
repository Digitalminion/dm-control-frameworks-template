## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-05 Outcome

**Lines of communication across the organization are established for cybersecurity risks, including risks from suppliers and other third parties**

Organizations establish comprehensive, multi-directional communication frameworks that enable effective sharing of cybersecurity risk information across all organizational levels and with external stakeholders, including suppliers and third parties, to support timely decision-making, coordinated response, and stakeholder awareness.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling timely and effective communication of cybersecurity risks across organizational boundaries
- Supporting coordinated decision-making and response through systematic information sharing
- Facilitating stakeholder awareness and engagement in cybersecurity risk management
- Enhancing organizational agility and responsiveness to emerging threats and incidents
- Demonstrating transparent risk communication for compliance and stakeholder confidence
