This template provides comprehensive guidance for implementing NIST CSF PR.DS-03, focusing on the formal management of assets throughout their entire lifecycle, including removal, transfers, and final disposition. Organizations must establish robust processes to ensure data security and compliance during all asset lifecycle transitions.
