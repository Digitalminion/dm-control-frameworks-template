## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-02 Outcome

**Risk appetite and risk tolerance statements are determined, communicated, and maintained**

Organizations establish clear risk appetite and tolerance statements that define the boundaries and levels of risk the organization is willing to accept to achieve its mission objectives and support strategic decision-making across all cybersecurity activities.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Providing clear boundaries for acceptable risk levels across all business operations
- Enabling consistent risk-based decision making throughout the organization
- Supporting strategic resource allocation aligned with risk appetite
- Facilitating stakeholder communication about cybersecurity risk acceptance
- Creating measurable risk tolerance thresholds for operational guidance
