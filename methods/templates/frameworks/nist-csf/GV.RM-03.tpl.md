## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-03 Outcome

**Enterprise risk management processes include cybersecurity risk management activities and outcomes**

Organizations integrate cybersecurity risk management into enterprise risk management processes to ensure cybersecurity risks are considered alongside other business risks in strategic planning, resource allocation, and organizational decision-making.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity risks are considered in enterprise decision-making
- Providing holistic view of organizational risk exposure and management
- Enabling coordinated risk response and resource allocation
- Supporting integrated risk reporting and communication
- Facilitating comprehensive risk governance and oversight
