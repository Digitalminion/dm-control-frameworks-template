## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OC-02 Outcome

**Internal and external stakeholders are understood, and their needs and expectations regarding cybersecurity risk management are understood and considered**

Organizations identify, understand, and actively engage with all relevant stakeholders to ensure cybersecurity risk management decisions consider and address stakeholder needs, expectations, and requirements.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity decisions consider all relevant stakeholder perspectives
- Building stakeholder trust and confidence through inclusive governance approaches
- Aligning cybersecurity investments with stakeholder value expectations
- Facilitating effective communication and buy-in for cybersecurity initiatives
- Supporting sustainable cybersecurity programs through stakeholder engagement
