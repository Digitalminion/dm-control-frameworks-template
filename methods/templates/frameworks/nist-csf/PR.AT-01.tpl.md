## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### PR.AT-01 Outcome

**All users are informed and trained**

Organizations implement comprehensive cybersecurity awareness and training programs to ensure all users understand their cybersecurity responsibilities, recognize threats, and follow appropriate security practices to protect organizational assets and information.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Building organizational cybersecurity culture and awareness
- Reducing human error and security incidents
- Enabling users to recognize and respond to cybersecurity threats
- Supporting compliance with regulatory training requirements
- Enhancing overall organizational security posture through informed users
