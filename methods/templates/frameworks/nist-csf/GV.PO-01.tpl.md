### GV.PO-01 Outcome

**Organizational cybersecurity policy is established and communicated**

Organizations develop, implement, and maintain comprehensive cybersecurity policies that define the organization's approach to cybersecurity risk management, establish governance frameworks, and provide clear guidance for cybersecurity decision-making throughout the organization.
