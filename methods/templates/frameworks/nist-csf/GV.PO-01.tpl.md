## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.PO-01 Outcome

**Organizational cybersecurity policy is established and communicated**

Organizations develop, implement, and maintain comprehensive cybersecurity policies that define the organization's approach to cybersecurity risk management, establish governance frameworks, and provide clear guidance for cybersecurity decision-making throughout the organization.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Establishing clear cybersecurity governance and accountability framework
- Providing consistent direction for cybersecurity decision-making
- Ensuring regulatory and compliance requirement alignment
- Supporting organizational risk management and strategic objectives
- Facilitating stakeholder communication and expectation management
