## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OC-05 Outcome

**Outcomes, capabilities, and services that the organization depends on are understood and communicated**

Organizations identify, assess, and clearly communicate their external dependencies to ensure supply chain relationships are understood and cybersecurity protections account for dependency risks and resilience requirements.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling comprehensive supply chain risk assessment and management
- Providing clear visibility into external dependency vulnerabilities and single points of failure
- Supporting business continuity planning through dependency understanding
- Facilitating vendor and supplier cybersecurity requirements development
- Informing resilience strategy through dependency criticality assessment
