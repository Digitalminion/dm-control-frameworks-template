### Executive Summary
This template provides comprehensive guidance for implementing NIST CSF RS.MI-01, focusing on establishing robust incident containment capabilities to limit the impact of cybersecurity incidents and prevent further damage. Organizations must implement rapid response containment procedures that can quickly isolate affected systems, prevent incident spread, and minimize business disruption while maintaining detailed documentation for investigation and recovery activities.
