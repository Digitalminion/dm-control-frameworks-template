## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OV-02 Outcome

**The cybersecurity risk management strategy is reviewed and adjusted to ensure coverage of organizational requirements and risks**

Organizations establish comprehensive strategy review processes to systematically assess cybersecurity risk management strategy coverage, identify gaps in organizational requirements and risk protection, and implement strategic adjustments to maintain comprehensive and effective risk management coverage.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity strategies comprehensively address all organizational requirements and risks
- Maintaining strategic alignment with evolving organizational needs and threat landscape
- Supporting proactive identification and remediation of strategy coverage gaps
- Enabling continuous strategic improvement and optimization
- Demonstrating systematic approach to strategic risk management governance
