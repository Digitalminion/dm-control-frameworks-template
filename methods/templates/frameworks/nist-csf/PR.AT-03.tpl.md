## NIST CSF Subcategory: PR.AT-03

### NIST CSF Subcategory Statement
**Senior executives understand their roles and responsibilities**

### Subcategory Purpose
This subcategory ensures that senior executives and organizational leadership understand their cybersecurity roles, responsibilities, and accountability, while also establishing appropriate security awareness and training for third-party personnel who have access to organizational systems and data.

### Implementation Approach

#### Tier 1 (Partial): Basic Executive Awareness
- **Executive Engagement**: Basic cybersecurity briefings for senior leadership
- **Third-Party Awareness**: Limited security orientation for external personnel
- **Accountability Framework**: Informal accountability and reporting structures
- **Documentation**: Basic policies outlining executive roles and third-party requirements

#### Tier 2 (Risk Informed): Risk-Based Leadership Training
- **Executive Engagement**: Risk-informed cybersecurity education for leadership
- **Third-Party Awareness**: Risk-based security training for external personnel
- **Accountability Framework**: Formal accountability structures with risk considerations
- **Documentation**: Risk-informed governance framework with defined accountabilities

#### Tier 3 (Repeatable): Standardized Governance Training
- **Executive Engagement**: Standardized cybersecurity governance education program
- **Third-Party Awareness**: Comprehensive security training for all external personnel
- **Accountability Framework**: Standardized accountability and oversight mechanisms
- **Documentation**: Comprehensive governance training program with measurable outcomes

#### Tier 4 (Adaptive): Advanced Leadership Development
- **Executive Engagement**: Adaptive cybersecurity leadership development program
- **Third-Party Awareness**: Dynamic security training adapted to third-party roles and risks
- **Accountability Framework**: Advanced accountability with continuous monitoring and adjustment
- **Documentation**: Advanced adaptive governance framework with continuous improvement
