## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RR-04 Outcome

**Cybersecurity is included in human resources practices**

Organizations systematically integrate cybersecurity considerations into all human resources practices, including personnel screening, onboarding, training, performance management, change notification, and offboarding processes, to support organizational security culture and mitigate human-related cybersecurity risks.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Establishing comprehensive human-centered cybersecurity risk management throughout employee lifecycle
- Building and maintaining strong organizational security culture and awareness
- Mitigating insider threats and human-related security risks through systematic HR integration
- Supporting compliance with employment, privacy, and security regulatory requirements
- Enabling effective cybersecurity capability development and sustainability through human capital management
