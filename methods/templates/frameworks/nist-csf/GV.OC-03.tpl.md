## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OC-03 Outcome

**Legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements regarding cybersecurity - including privacy and civil liberties obligations - are understood and managed**

Organizations identify, understand, and actively manage all applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual cybersecurity requirements to ensure compliance and reduce legal and business risks.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity programs meet all applicable legal and regulatory requirements
- Reducing legal, financial, and reputational risks from non-compliance
- Establishing clear compliance frameworks for cybersecurity decision-making
- Supporting business operations through proactive compliance management
- Building stakeholder trust through demonstrated regulatory adherence
