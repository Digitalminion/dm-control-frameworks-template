## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-07 Outcome

**Strategic opportunities (i.e., positive risks) are characterized and are included in organizational cybersecurity risk discussions**

Organizations systematically identify, characterize, and evaluate cybersecurity-related strategic opportunities (positive risks) and integrate them into organizational risk management discussions and decision-making processes to maximize value creation and competitive advantage from cybersecurity investments and capabilities.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Identifying and leveraging cybersecurity investments for competitive advantage and value creation
- Balancing risk management discussions to include both threats and opportunities
- Supporting strategic decision-making through comprehensive risk-opportunity analysis
- Enabling innovation and growth through cybersecurity-enabled business opportunities
- Demonstrating cybersecurity value beyond risk mitigation to stakeholders and leadership
