## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RM-06 Outcome

**A standardized method for calculating, documenting, categorizing, and prioritizing cybersecurity risks is established and communicated**

Organizations establish comprehensive, standardized methodologies for cybersecurity risk calculation, documentation, categorization, and prioritization that enable consistent assessment across the enterprise, effective risk aggregation and comparison, and informed decision-making at all organizational levels.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling consistent and comparable risk assessment across all organizational units and functions
- Supporting effective enterprise risk aggregation and prioritization for strategic decision-making
- Facilitating clear risk communication and understanding among stakeholders at all levels
- Providing foundation for evidence-based resource allocation and investment decisions
- Demonstrating systematic approach to risk management for compliance and audit purposes
