## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.RR-01 Outcome

**Organizational leadership is responsible and accountable for cybersecurity risk and fosters a culture that is risk-aware, ethical, and continually improving**

Organizations establish clear leadership accountability for cybersecurity risk management and create a culture where cybersecurity is integrated into organizational values, decision-making processes, and continuous improvement activities.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Establishing clear executive accountability for cybersecurity outcomes
- Creating a risk-aware culture throughout the organization
- Integrating cybersecurity into strategic decision-making processes
- Promoting ethical behavior and responsible risk management
- Enabling continuous improvement in cybersecurity capabilities
