## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.PO-02 Outcome

**Policy for managing cybersecurity risks is reviewed, updated, communicated, and enforced to reflect changes in requirements, threats, technology, and organizational mission**

Organizations establish comprehensive policy lifecycle management processes to ensure cybersecurity risk management policies remain current, effective, and aligned with changing requirements, evolving threats, technological advances, and organizational mission evolution.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Ensuring cybersecurity policies remain relevant and effective in dynamic environments
- Maintaining alignment between policies and organizational objectives, regulatory requirements, and threat landscape
- Supporting consistent policy application and enforcement across the organization
- Enabling rapid adaptation to changes in requirements, threats, and technology
- Demonstrating commitment to continuous improvement and regulatory compliance
