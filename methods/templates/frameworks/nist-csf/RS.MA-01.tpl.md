## RISK CONSIDERATIONS

### Threat Landscape
- **Coordination Failures:** Risk of ineffective stakeholder coordination during incidents
- **Communication Breakdown:** Potential for communication failures and misinformation
- **Resource Conflicts:** Competition for resources during multiple incident responses
- **Third-Party Dependencies:** Over-reliance on external parties for incident response
- **Escalation Delays:** Potential delays in incident escalation and authority notification

### Mitigation Strategies
- Implement comprehensive incident coordination backup and redundancy systems
- Deploy robust communication protocols and multi-channel coordination capabilities
- Establish clear roles, responsibilities, and authority structures for incident response
- Create diversified incident response capabilities to reduce external dependencies
- Implement automated escalation and notification systems for critical incidents
