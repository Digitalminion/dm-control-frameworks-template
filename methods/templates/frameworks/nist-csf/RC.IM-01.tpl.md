## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### RC.IM-01 Outcome

**Recovery activities are incorporated into lessons learned**

Organizations systematically capture, analyze, and integrate lessons learned from recovery activities to continuously improve recovery capabilities, processes, and procedures, enabling enhanced organizational resilience and more effective future recovery operations.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Enabling continuous improvement of recovery capabilities and processes
- Facilitating organizational learning from recovery experiences
- Supporting enhanced resilience and preparedness for future incidents
- Improving recovery efficiency and effectiveness over time
- Creating knowledge base for organizational recovery maturity
