## NIST CSF Subcategory Statement

### GV.OV-03 Outcome

**Organizational cybersecurity risk management performance is measured and reviewed for adjustments needed**

Organizations establish comprehensive performance measurement systems to monitor cybersecurity risk management effectiveness, analyze performance trends and indicators, and systematically review results to identify and implement necessary adjustments for continuous improvement.

### Strategic Value

This subcategory supports organizational cybersecurity by:
- Providing quantifiable evidence of cybersecurity risk management effectiveness and efficiency
- Enabling data-driven decision making for resource allocation and strategic adjustments
- Supporting accountability and transparency to stakeholders and leadership
- Facilitating continuous improvement in cybersecurity risk management capabilities
- Ensuring alignment between cybersecurity performance and organizational objectives
